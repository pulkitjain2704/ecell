HELLO! WELCOME TO Internship Fair 2019

One of the Biggest Internship Fair

6 April, 2019. New Delhi

About The Internship Fair 2019

Be the part of grand event of Internship Fair 2019 to be organized by E-Cell ADGITM. An event orchestrated for fulfilling the dreams of every worthy candidate out there.

Get a coveted internships in your dream companies and earn a handsome stipend. Last year 1500 people from institutions like DU, DTU, NSIT, IIT and IPU witnessed 35 companies select and honor 400 capable candidates.

This is an opportunity for candidates from all the colleges of Delhi-NCR to prove their metal in the real corporate world . Internships will be provided for all the job profiles i.e. tech and non-tech both.

The biggest Internship Fair of Delhi-NCR awaits you on 6 April 2019 at Akhilesh Das Gupta Institute of Technology and Management , 10 am onwards.
